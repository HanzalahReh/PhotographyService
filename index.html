Wedding Photography

Special Services for your Special Day.

We are a group of professionals that are not limited to DSLR cameras for taking photos, but we are also commercially licensed to use drones to take aerial photos that would otherwise not be possible for a low price.

We use only the best drones that are mounted with an additional DSLR camera so there is no compromise in the quality of the image
We offer competitive prices compared to our competitors and will provide a better package than our competitors.

Standard package(Recommended for medium size wedding of 20-50 people)
Will include 8 hours of photography using DSLR cameras and a drone with 2 photographers on site, a 20 page album of 14 inches by 12 inches, engagement photo session, and lighting equipment. The price of all this will be $3,500. We will provide a list of available photographers for your selected date and their past works. We will then have your meet up with them before the actual photoshoot to discuss the plan and layout of the shoots.
Base package(Recommended for small size weddings of 20 or less)
Will include a photographer using a drone or a camera. The price is $500 hour with included lightning and a 15 page album of 14 by 12 inches. Meet up with photograph of your choosing is also included.
VIP Package(Recommended for large size weddings of 50+)
Will include 4 photographers with 2 cameras and 2 drones. They will be able to cover the entire event for as long as it goes. Includes complementary 32 x 24 inch wedding poster of the bride and groom. A 40 page album 14x 12 of the entire event will be included. Price is $10,000
A flash drive of all the photos and videos recorded during the event will be sent to the bride and groom for them to select which photos they would like to be put into the album. The flash drive is to be kept and not returned.
